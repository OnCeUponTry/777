# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) in spirit:

- Be respectful. Disagree on technical merits without personal attacks.
- Assume good faith. Most miscommunications are honest mistakes.
- Keep discussions focused on the technical work.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, doxing, sustained disruption of discussions, and any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

If you experience or witness behavior that violates these expectations, please open a confidential issue or contact the maintainer directly. The maintainer reserves the right to remove comments, lock threads, ban participants, or take other actions necessary to keep this project usable.

This is a small alpha-stage project maintained by one person. Disagreements are normal; the goal is to keep the technical signal high.
